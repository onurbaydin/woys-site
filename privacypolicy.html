Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Woys AI is operated by Buyxall LLC, registered in Sheridan, Wyoming, USA. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Nature of the Service

Woys AI analyzes voluntarily submitted audio recordings to estimate emotional patterns, stress indicators, and vocal inconsistencies using our proprietary machine learning models. The service does not perform biometric identification or attempt to determine factual truth with absolute certainty.

2. Data Collection & Usage

  • Audio Data: Processed solely to generate analysis results. Stored securely and deleted after 30 days or when storage limits (10 analyses) are exceeded.
  • Contact Info: Names and email addresses collected via Apple/Google Sign-In are used for authentication and account management.
  • Identifiers: User IDs are used to link your analyses and subscriptions to your account.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs are collected to ensure app stability and performance.

3. Data Deletion & Control

You have full control over your data. You may delete any analysis at any time from within the app. Deletion permanently removes the associated audio and analysis results from our active cloud storage.

4. Accuracy Disclaimer

AI-driven vocal analysis is probabilistic. Results are estimates and should not be used as forensic evidence, legal proof, or in critical decision-making processes such as employment or medical evaluations.

5. Security

We use private cloud infrastructure and industry-standard encryption to protect your data. We never sell your personal information or audio data to third parties.
